Our development teams ensure that clients are promptly informed on progress changes made to their work.

Our senior engineers attend workshops both locally and abroad in order to ensure reliability of our systems and to keep upwith modern trends in this dynamic computer age.

At QET Systems, we strive to go the extra mile for our clients. We incorporate rich, intuitive user interfaces and simplified control logic to ensure a seamless user experience.

Our Story

We are a software company based in Nairobi and Thika,Kenya. Founded in 2010, and incorporated in 2015 our prime focus is on ICT services which include:

  • Design and building of web based transactional systems
  • Technical assessment
  • Networking
  • Network design and implementation
  • ICT consulting
  • Embedded systems programming
  • Industrial PLC and SCADA systems implementation The founding partners are a group of Electronic and Computer engineers from JKUAT with diverse specialties like cyber security, system optimization and communication design.
